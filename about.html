Who We Are

About the Awards

Our Mission

The Socratic Awards was founded with a singular purpose: to recognize literature that doesn't just tell stories, but changes the way readers see the world. We believe books have the power to level understanding, empathy, and human connection across all walks of life.

Each year, our panel of judges — drawn from authors, critics, librarians, and passionate readers — evaluates hundreds of submissions across multiple categories to identify the works that truly rise above.

Our History

In our inaugural year, we received over 300 submissions across three categories, and the response from the literary community has been humbling. We look forward to growing the awards and deepening our commitment to exceptional writing.

The Judging Process

All submitted works are read in full by at least two judges. Longlists are compiled in January, shortlists announced in March, and winners revealed in May. Judges deliberate independently before convening to reach a consensus, ensuring each decision is the result of genuine literary discussion.
